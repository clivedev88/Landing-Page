After that, I pursued a career as a teacher. I worked at language schools like Wizard (2016 - 2020), Effort Idiomas (2017), and Espaço Aberto (2019). In 2023, I decided to return to studying and started an Online Graduation in Systems Analysis and Development at Faculdade Estácio. To get more practical experience, I also enrolled in a Full Stack Programming Course at Infinity School in Fortaleza. My goal is to fully transition to the programming area. Currently, I'm enrolled in a course at Alura where I aim to study everything about programming and design.

Over the past year, I've been working and studying diligently. I've learned languages like Python, HTML, CSS, and gained experience with frameworks like FLASK, Django, and databases like MySQL. I'm currently learning JavaScript and continuously expanding my knowledge. You can check out my projects on my GitHub account.
